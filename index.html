Where the Lake
Meets the Bar

Cabin 12 sits right on the water in Greenwood Lake, NY. Pull up a stool, watch the lake, and stay a while. We're a neighborhood bar and kitchen built for locals and visitors who know that the best views come with a cold drink.

From lakeside bites to craft cocktails and cold drafts, everything here is made to match the easy pace of life on the water.
